About Me

Hello! My name is Andrea Benavides Aguirre! I am an enthusiastic third year Software Engineering student at the Schulich School of Engineering at the University of Calgary!


My mission is to create impactful technology that can make a positive difference. I strive to continually expand my learning so that I can be a creative and effective innovator.


Outside of developing, my passions lie with volunteerism, travelling, language learning, reading and music! I love spending my free time working towards causes that I believe in, particularly the advancement of education in underserved groups.


I find that travelling is one of the ultimate ways of cultivating an open mind and engaging in enriching and rewarding experiences. Click on the paper airplane below to see where I've had the privilege of visiting!


Along with my love of travel, I have a fondness for language learning. I have had the fantastic opportunity to learn three languages and I'm working on my fourth!
